You may notice that depending on the way the neural net creates a model of the data, the estimated emissions values may dramatically over or under estimate. Remember, this is a model based on a model so errors will propograte up. This example is to showcase the capability to train neural nets in the browser with tensorflow.js as well as how we might approach data critically if/when we notice strange or unexpected behaviors.
